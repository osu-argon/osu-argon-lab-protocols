## Acid Leach Log Sheet

## ID

`acid_leach_sheet`

## Requires


## Description

Acid leaching is conducted in LDPE bottles. The bottles have pre-assigned numbers and the sample name is not put on the bottles, but assigned a number. The sample name is written next to the number of the available bottle.

## Procedure

1. Gather samples that will be leached.
2. Gather the same amount of LDPE bottles that are appropriatly sized for the amount of sample that will be leached.
3. Assign a number to a sample and write it down on the acid leaching log.
4. Check that the number and the assigned sample are correct.
