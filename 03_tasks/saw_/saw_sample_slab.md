## Protocol for slabbing a sample

## ID

`saw_sample_slab`

## Requires

`saw_prep`

## Description

Use a saw to cut 1-2 inch thick “slabs” of a whole rock sample.

## Procedure

1. `saw_prep`
2. Cut sample so that you can obtain a flat stable surface.
3. Use the ruler on the saw table to estimate where to cut the sample.
4. Continue until the sample is finished.
