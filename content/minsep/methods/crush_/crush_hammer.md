# Hammer Crush

## ID

`crush_hammer`

## Requires

`workspace_prep`

## Description

The hammer crush method is used to break down large pieces of a sample in order to make it an appropriate size for the small or large jaw crusher.

## Procedure

- `workspace_prep`
- Take a single piece of rock and wrap it in at least two pieces of 8x11 inch office paper.
- Place another piece of paper over the steel plate. This will catch debris after crushing.
- Place the steel sample holder on the steel plate.
- Place the sample wrapped in paper insixe the sample holder.
- Use a handseld sledge hammer to break the sample into smaller pieces.
- Carefully remove the steel sample holder and becareful to collect the crushed material.
- Repeat the process if necessary.
