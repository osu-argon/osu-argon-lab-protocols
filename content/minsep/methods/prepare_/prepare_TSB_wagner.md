# Petrographic Thin Sections: Prep and Submitting an order

Method for preparing a thin section billet. To be used for preparing a billet to be sent to Wagner Petrographic or to client.

## ID

`prepare_TSB`

## Requires

`saw_prep`
`saw_sample_slab`

## Description

Rock is slabbed into two inch slabs using a diamond rimmed saw. A representative 2x2x3 inch piece of the rock.

## Procedure

1. Slab rock into two inch thick slices.
1. Remove any visible weathering rinds.
1. Identify region of slab that best represents the composition of the rock. Outline with marker.
1. Determine Dimensions of Desired Thin Section
    - Standard: 24x46 mm [0.9x1.8 in]
    - Large : 76x51 mm [3x2 in]
1. Use a rock saw extract a piece that is roughly 2-3x bigger than the required thin section.
1. Place piece into 6 mil bag. Write the sample identifier on both sides.
1. Place Tyvek tag with sample identifier in the with sample.

Samples are sent to Wagner Petrographic for thin sections [http://wagnerpetrographic.com/].
