Pack samples for Irradiation
============================

**ID**

`irradiate_pack`

**Requires**


**Description**

Procedure to pack picked samples for irradiation.

**Procedure**

1. Weigh samples to the desired amount to irradiate. Generally enough for two analyses.
2. Put pre-made aluminum capsule in the pack plate.
3. Use funnel to pour sample into capsule.
4. Clip top of the capsule to leave a few millimeters of aluminum to fold over the material.
5. Make four folds over the material and use the streel stamp tool to mould the capsule.
