### crush

- [crush_jaw_large](https://github.com/danielheaton/argonlabprotocols/blob/master/02_methods/crush_/crush_jaw_large.md)
    - For large amounts of sample.
- [crush_jaw_small](https://github.com/danielheaton/argonlabprotocols/blob/master/02_methods/crush_/crush_jaw_small.md)
    - For small amounts of sample or when more crushing control is needed.
- [crush_hammer](https://github.com/danielheaton/argonlabprotocols/blob/master/02_methods/crush_/crush_hammer.md)
    - To break up large pieces of sample for use in the small or large jaw crusher.
- [crush_mortar_pestle](https://github.com/danielheaton/argonlabprotocols/blob/master/02_methods/crush_/crush_mortar_pestle.md)
    - To use when the sample amount is small or finer control over the crushing of the sample is needed.

### prepare

- [prepare_slab](https://github.com/danielheaton/argonlabprotocols/blob/master/02_methods/prepare_/prepare_slab.md)
    - Sample needs to be cut into 1-inch slabs before the sample is crushed. Saw away weathering rind if possible.
- [prepare_TSB](https://github.com/danielheaton/argonlabprotocols/blob/master/02_methods/prepare_/prepare_TSB.md)
    - Sample is to be slabbed and a portion selected to be sent for a thin section.
- [prepare_TSB_XRF](https://github.com/danielheaton/argonlabprotocols/blob/master/02_methods/prepare_/prepare_TSB_XRF.md)
    - Thin section to be sent and a portion of the sample is to be prepared for XRF.
- [prepare_XRF](https://github.com/danielheaton/argonlabprotocols/blob/master/02_methods/prepare_/prepare_XRF.md)
    - A portion of the sample needs to be prepared and sent for XRF analysis.
